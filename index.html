Transparent Rental Terms

Professional policies built on trust and clarity.

Late Returns

Vehicles must be returned at the agreed time. Late return charge: ₹200 per hour (Bike) / ₹500 per hour (Car). 30-min grace allowed.

Fuel Policy

We provide a full/partial tank. Fuel level must match pickup level at return. If lower, actual fuel cost + service fee applies.

Damage Rules

Minor scratches are inspected fairly. Any major damage or part replacement costs will be charged as per official authorized showroom estimates.

Refunds

Cancellation before 24 hours: 100% refund. Within 12 hours: 50% refund. No refund after pickup or for no-shows.

KYC Mandatory

Original Driving License (DL) and Aadhar Card are mandatory. We perform digital verification before handing over the keys.
